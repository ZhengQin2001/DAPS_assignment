priceEarningsRatio: Provides a valuation metric comparing the company's share price to its earnings per share.
netProfitMargin: Represents how much net income is generated as a percentage of revenues.
quickRatio: Similar to the current ratio but excludes inventory from current assets.
interestCoverage: Measures a company's ability to make interest payments on its debt.
dividendYield: Indicates the company’s dividend as a percentage of its stock price.
priceToBookRatio: Compares the company's market value to its book value.
enterpriseValueMultiple: Reflects a company's total value relative to key financial metrics like EBITDA.
effectiveTaxRate: The average rate at which a corporation is taxed.
returnOnAssets: Shows how profitable a company's assets are in generating revenue.
cashFlowToDebtRatio: Indicates the company's ability to cover its debt with its cash flow.
payoutRatio: The proportion of earnings paid out as dividends to shareholders.
ebtPerEbit: Reflects profitability before tax and interest as a proportion of overall earnings.
debtRatio: Measures the extent of a company’s leverage in relation to its assets.
companyEquityMultiplier: Indicates the company's financial leverage, calculated as total assets divided by total equity.
receivablesTurnover: Measures how efficiently a company uses its assets.
priceToSalesRatio: A valuation ratio that compares a company’s stock price to its revenues.
